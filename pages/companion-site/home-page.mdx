# Home Page

Within the Home Page modal, you can configure the experience of the [Home page](/companion-site/pages/home) of your site, including:

- **Site Description** - This is the site description listed on the Home page.
- **Banner Image** - The image banner at the top of the Home page. For the best results, please use an image with dimensions of at least 827 x 192. Very tall images may be clipped.
- **Enabled Components** - Enable or disable the components displayed on the Home page, including:
  - Leaderboard
  - Feeds Tile Menu
  - Upcoming Events List
