# Community Images

Within the Community Images modal, you can configure the following images associated with your community's brand within the Slack workspace:

- Community Horizontal Logo
- Community Icon

For more information on the specs and recommendations of these images, see the [Branding Guide](/companion-site/branding#image-assets).
