# Leaderboard & Directory Pages

Under the Members page, you can find the community **leaderboard** and **directory**.
The page will display the number of members in the Tightknit companion site, which is linked to the number of users in the Slack workspace.

## Leaderboard

The leaderboard displays the top most active users in the community ranked by a gamification score of their combined posts and comments.

![Tightknit Companion Site Leaderboard](/tightknit-site/leaderboard.png)

<Callout type="info">
  Up to a max of 10 users are displayed on the leaderboard.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
  By default, bot and admin users are excluded from the leaderboard. Inclusion
  of admins can be configured in the [Tightknit app member
  settings](../slack-administration/member-settings).
</Callout>

## Directory

The directory lists a selection of member profiles within the Tightknit companion site.

The number of profiles displayed can be configured in the [Tightknit app member settings](../slack-administration/member-settings).

![Tightknit Companion Site Directory](/tightknit-site/directory.png)
