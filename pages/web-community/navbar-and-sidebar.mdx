# Navbar & Sidebar

## Navbar Header

The navbar header will contain your community logo and name, and the call-to-action button.

![Community navbar header](/web-community/navbar.png)

The call-to-action button is typically a link to join your Slack community.

## Sidebar

The navigation sidebar contains links to all [feeds](./feed.mdx), except unlisted feeds, as well as the following items:

- [Home](./home.mdx)
- [Members](./leaderboard-directory.mdx)

![Community sidebar](/web-community/sidebar.png)
