# Introduction

🧶 Tightknit helps businesses grow, manage, and service their customer
communities with Slack and AI.

![Tightknit Site](/web-community-screenshot.png)

While Slack is one of the absolute best tools for building a community, it's not without its challenges. Tightknit is your trusted Slack community partner to keep your community healthy, growing, and engaged.

Use our **Slack tools** to learn how your members use the community, and get insightful analytics of workspace activity. Read to take your community to the next level? Spin up a **companion website** that unlocks the treasure trove of your conversations to the searchable web and drives traffic to your workspace, all _without ever leaving Slack!_

A shortlist of some of our most loved site features include:

- 🪄 SEO powered by AI
- 📅 Retain messages past Slack 90 day limit
- 🌐 Landing page
- 😎 Customer profiles
- 🥇 Leaderboards
- 🧵 RSS feeds

Ready to learn more? We're excited to take you on the Tightknit journey!
